Photography

A picture book of all my photography projects!

Intro

My time in photography is more brief than in other areas, however I have a strong working knowledge of photography theory from several photography classes as well as many trips to take photos in downtown Toronto. Most of the images I take are edited in a mix of Adobe Lightroom and Adobe Photoshop, and are taken using Canon cameras, primarily a 60D or Rebel T5i.

Portrait Shots

These photos were taken in December of 2017 as a final project in a Photography class. The photos were taken using a Nikon D700 as well as using an LED lighting kit while in a studio. The photos were meant to be about self reflection.

These photos were solely edited using Adobe Photoshop, where I initially corrected the lighting and contrast before turning the images into black and white. After that I used the Spot Healing tool for minor touch ups.

All images used in this set are original images taken and edited by me.
