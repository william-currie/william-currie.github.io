About Me

Hello! My name is William Currie, and I am a Digital Designer! My path of being a designer began in highschool, when I was constantly finding myself taking art-oriented classes, many of which on the digital side. For four years of highschool, I kept myself busy on Adobe Photoshop, Premiere Pro and Animate (or as it was known then, Adobe Flash). This practice ended up leading me to the University of Guelph-Humber, where I still study today. My program offers a wide education option media, and teaches us about all the workings of the media industry and how they intertwine before allowing us to specialize in our chosen field. This gives us a unique edge by helping us have a full understanding of the different roles within our industry before helping us become experts in our chosen area, mine being Digital Communications. This specialization of Digital Communications has given me the knowledge of how to design, setup and structure websites as well as make much of the digital content for the websites, ranging from logos to advertisement videos to photography, all while keeping in mind the company brand and image to better represent my clients. If you are looking for a passionate, hard-working and fast-learner who can create your image online, you have found the right person!
